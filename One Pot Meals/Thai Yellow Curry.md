### Ingredients
65g mae ploy paste
2 cans coconut milk
sugar
ginger
garlic

2 tsp corn starch

tofu

**Vegetables**
zucchini
carrots
potatoes
mushrooms
bell pepper
corn
spinach
broccoli



### Instructions
Cook garlic, ginger, veggies in oil
add paste and fry
add coconut milk
add sugar
Thicken with corn starch
