### Ingredients
Dal:
1 cup (200g) toor dal
3 cups water

1 tbsp oil
1/2 cup onion (half)
1/4 tsp cumin powder
4 cloves garlic
1 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1 cup tomatoes
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
2 tsp dried coriander powder
1 medium lemon

Tadka:
2 tbsp ghee
1/4 tsp cumin powder
1 tbsp garlic
1/8 tsp hing
1/4 tsp red chili powder
1/8 tsp mustard powder (optional)


### Directions
Aromatics:
1. Heat oil in pan (or in instant pot)
2. Add cumin and onions, saute until slightly golden
3. Add ginger and garlic, saute on low heat until fragrant
4. Add chili powder, turmeric, and garam masala, give a quick stir
5. Stir in tomatoes and salt, until tomatoes are soft
6. Transfer to cooked dal and simmer for a few minutes. Taste test, check if salt is needed.
7. Add fenugreek and coriander, turn off heat

Dal:
1. Rinse dal until water is clear
2. Add dal and water to instant pot. Cook for 10 minutes on high pressure. This can be added to the cooked tomatoes and aromatics

Tadka
1. Heat 2 tbsp ghee on medium heat
2. Add cumin and mustard seed powder, let them fry on low heat
3. Add garlic. Fry until aromatic, do not brown.
4. Stir in hing and red chili powder. Quickly remove from heat and stir into dal.
