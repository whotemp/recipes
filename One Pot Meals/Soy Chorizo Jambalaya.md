## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup dry chickpeas (or 1 can of any bean)
- 2 cups long-grain rice
- 2 cans 14.5 oz diced tomatoes
- 2.5 cups vegetable stock
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 1 medium bell pepper, diced
- 3 celery ribs, diced
- 2 zucchini, diced
- some mushrooms, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 12 oz Trader Joe's soy chorizo
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 2 bay leaves (optional)


## Instructions
1. Soak chickpeas overnight, cook for 10 minutes high pressure in instant pot
2. Rinse rice
3. Prep veggies, measure out herbs and spices into bowl
4. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C)
5. In Dutch oven, cook onion, bell pepper, celery, zucchini, mushroom, and garlic
6. Add in herbs and spices
7. Add in soy chorizo and chickpeas
8. Add tomatoes, stock, and bay leaves and bring to a simmer. Season with salt, tasting liquid to ensure it is well seasoned.
9. Stir in rice and return to a simmer. Cover with lid and transfer to oven. Bake 40 minutes until liquid is fully absorbed and rice is tender.
