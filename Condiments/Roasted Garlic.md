### Ingredients
- garlic
- olive oil

### Instructions
1. Cut bottom off of garlic, keep cloves in shell
2. Place garlic upside down in aluminum foil
3. Coat garlic in bowl of olive oil
4. Roast at 400F for 18 minutes
