## Ingredients

Base
- 2 cans white beans
- 1/2 large onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- salt and (white) pepper to taste
- vinegar to taste

Optional
- 1 large carrot
- 2 stalks celery
- frozen chopped spinach
- mushrooms
- potatoes
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- sundried tomatoes
- parmesan cheese


## Notes
