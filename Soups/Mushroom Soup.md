### Ingredients
- Olive oil
- 1 large onion
- 4 cloves garlic
- 24 oz mushrooms
- 1 carrot
- cayenne pepper
- salt and pepper
- 1/4 cup flour
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- 1 tsp dried sage
- 1 box trader joe's condensed mushroom

### Instructions
1. To one large pot, add in the onion and carrot, saute for 5 minutes. Stir in the garlic for 30 seconds. Add in the mushrooms, salt and pepper to taste (and cayenne if using). Stir it all to saute for 10 minutes.
2. Next, sprinkle on the flour and stir to coat all. Pour in the broth, soy sauce and thyme and bring to a boil. Lower the heat to low and simmer COVERED, for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Blend soup with immersion blender
