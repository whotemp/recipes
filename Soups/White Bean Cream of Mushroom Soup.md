## Ingredients
- 1 can cream of mushroom
- 1 can white beans (drained)
- 2 cups broth
- 1 cup spinach
- 1 small onion
- ½ tsp paprika, ¼ tsp thyme
- Optional: cheddar or parmesan

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion until soft.
2. Add beans + broth and simmer 5 minutes.
3. Stir in cream of mushroom.
4. Add spinach, paprika, thyme.
5. Simmer gently 3–4 minutes.
6. Add cheese if you want it extra comfort-y.
