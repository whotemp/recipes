## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 3 cubes ginger
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1/2 cup hot water (use peanut butter cup with cubes)
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- < 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 Tbsp sweetener
- sriracha to taste
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
