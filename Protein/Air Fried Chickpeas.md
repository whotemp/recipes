### Ingredients
chickpeas
salt
garlic powder
cumin
trader joe's mushroom umami seasoning

### Directions
1. soak chickpeas overnight
2. 20 minutes instant pot high
3. toss in olive oil and seasoning
4. 12 minutes air fryer at 400 degrees
