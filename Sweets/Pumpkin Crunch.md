## Ingredients
- 1 stick of butter (about 8 tablespoons)
- 1/2 box yellow cake mix (15.25 oz)
- 1 can of pumpkin (15 oz)
- 1 can evaporated milk (12 oz)
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Frosting
- One 8 oz. cream cheese
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 Tbsp vanilla (optional)

## Instructions
1. Chill whisk attachment and bowl.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3. Mix pumpkin, milk, eggs, cinnamon, sugar, and walnuts. Pour into pan.
4. Sprinkle half box of cake mix on top of pumpkin mix, spoon melted butter over top.
5. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. When cool, loosen from side of pan.
6. Whip cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla. Slowly add in heavy cream. Careful to not over mix!
7. Frost with frosting.
8. Cut into squares and serve.
